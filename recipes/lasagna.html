Lasagna Recipe

Description

This recipe will have the eaters begging for more!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup oil
  • your choice of veggies
  • black pepper
  • salt
  • 3 large cloves of fresh garlic, grated
  • 1 can of your favourite tomato sauce
  • 1lb of ground beef
  • 1 cup of fresh parsley leaves
  • 1 cup of your favourite grated cheeses mixed
  • 20 lasagna noodles
