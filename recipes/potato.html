Seasoned Roasted Potato Pieces Recipe

Description

One of your new fave potato dishes!

Ingredients

  • 5 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 medium sized irish potatoes
  • black pepper
  • salt
  • 1 large clove of fresh garlic, grated
  • 1/4 cup of fresh parsley leaves

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400degF
  2. Cut potatoes in your preferred size.
  3. Add potatoes in baking dish.
  4. Add other ingredients to the potato and mix until each potato is covered generously and evenly.
  5. Bake for about 45mins, or until potatoes are fully cooked.
  6. To make potatoes crispy, broil in oven for about 5mins or until your cripsy-ness preference.
