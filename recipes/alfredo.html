Lasagna Recipe

Description

I guarantee you've never had alfredo like this before!

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of fettuccine pasta
  • your choice of veggies
  • black pepper
  • salt
  • 3 large cloves of fresh garlic, grated
  • 1 cup of evaporated milk
  • 1 cup of your favourite grated cheeses mixed
  • 1 tbsp of mustard
  • 1/3 cup of fresh parsley leaves
  • 1 cup Maggi all purpose seasoning
  • 1/4 stick of butter

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until half cooked.
  2. When pasta finishes, throw out most of the water.
  3. Bring down heat to simmer.
  4. Add all other ingredients to pasta that is in the pasta water.
  5. Stir constantly until it reaches your preferred consistancy.
